FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does the process work?
It's four simple steps: 1) Discovery Call — book a free consultation where we discuss your needs and determine the best solution. 2) Custom Proposal — you get a clear plan with timeline and cost, no surprises or hidden fees. 3) Build & Launch — we build your solution while keeping you updated, delivered exactly as needed. 4) Support & Optimization — ongoing support to keep your solution performing as you grow.
How much does a project cost?
Every project is different, so pricing depends on scope and complexity. We provide a clear, upfront quote after our discovery call — no hidden fees, no surprises. We work with budgets of all sizes.
Can you really deliver in 30 days?
Yes — for most projects. Our streamlined process and experienced team allow us to move fast without cutting corners. Larger enterprise projects may take longer, and we'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront.
Do you offer revisions?
Absolutely. We keep you involved throughout the build process with regular check-ins. Revisions and feedback are built into our workflow so the final product is exactly what you need.
What happens after launch?
We don't disappear after delivery. We offer ongoing support, maintenance, and optimization packages so your software stays fast, secure, and up-to-date as your business grows.
What if I'm not sure what I need yet?
That's exactly what the free consultation is for. Most of our clients come to us with a rough idea and we help them define the scope, features, and timeline. No preparation needed — just bring your idea.
Is there any commitment to get started?
None at all. The consultation is completely free with zero obligation. If we're a good fit, we'll send you a clear proposal. You decide if and when to move forward — no pressure, no follow-up calls you didn't ask for.
